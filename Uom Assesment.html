Beaches in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is a tropical island in the deep blue waters of the Indian ocean and the Bay of Bengal. The country is endowed with over a thousand miles of beautiful golden beaches that are fringed with coconut palms making it in the ideal destination for a beach holiday. A large percentage of luxurious resorts and villas as well as budget hotels are located along the coastal side to offer tourists some of the best beach holidays in Sri Lanka.
There are few famous beaches in Sri Lanka

  • Hikkaduwa
  • Nilaweli
  • Marble Beach
  • Unawatuna
